Events list

IMPORTANT : I will only talk about event that require specific conditions, scenes obtained through normal progression of the story that can't be missed won't be present here.

Candies / Chocolates

This item is used to unlock content for Alice.
You need to have relationship Warm or more with Lisa and she must have a good mood.
You'll be able to start a conversation with Lisa. You have a strange appearance.... You'll be able to purchase it once you pass the persuasion check.

Club before Kira

You need the candies for this event.
It's relatively hard to do in 0.3, you need to be able to purchase candies before Kira arrival. If you manage to do it, give candies to Alice - Friday 20:00. She'll be back at 03:00 in the bathroom. Use Knock.

Club After Kira

Give candies to alice (Friday - 20:00). She'll be back from club with Kira at 03:00.
You need 400 authority on Alice to continue.
You need to pass few persuasion check. Once you reach : You're a moron, Max ... Do you like it? you have 2 options.
1: Try persuasion check for blowjob. Feels great! Could you maybe ... kiss it? [skill.social_1]
2: Very! (cum) which is nice too (imo).

Dressing without panties

Only possible before Kira's arrival.
When you caught Alice smoking ask her to stop wearing panties, it will unlock few scenes : new spanking and new dressing on saturday & sunday.

Smoking topless

Just ask her to do so when you caught her smoking ;). Nothing else.

Shower - Spider event

Only possible before Kira's arrival.
You can throw a spider on Alice if you have one.

Night - Spider event

Hide a spider in her bed at midnight for maximum success rate. Don't kill the spider for nothing.

Sunbath - Spider event

You throw a spider on her when she's at the pool.
If you convinced her to be topless when you used sunblock, you can throw a spider at this moment and she'll be topless.

Eric's gift

Every wednesday at 20:00, Eric is in her room. There's 8 different scenes (you need 8 weeks to see them all).

Eric's massage

Every wednesday at 20:00 if Eric influence is > 59, he will give her a massage.
If his influence reach 100%, there's another scene.

Eric's punishment

If Eric's influence is > 69 and he's home when Alice should be spanked, you'll trigger a special event.

TV massage

You can give her candies before you start the massage to go further than usual.
How to reach end of scene: Yes, it will be for dessert... OR Continue to be silent THEN Continue to massage

Little black dress

Don't give it for nothing.

Spanking (mom)

You need the find cigarette in her room and set her up.

Spanking (Max)

When you caught her smoking you can ask for it, try all outcomes, they're not all the same.
