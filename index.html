Intro


The walkthrough is not complete but you can find lots of info.


0.3 changed quite a few things and a step by step walkthrough is too time consuming to do.
My approach changed, i'll list events that require trigger or are hidden and tell you how to unlock them.
I'll also give you what's the best order (in my opinion) to do the events to avoid minimum content loss in one playthrough and when to save to avoid to start from scratch.

Events order

Day 1 - 10 Road to 400$


There's only one important thing : DO NOT TALK TO LISA ABOUT ALEX WHEN THE CONVERSATION START WITH As for Alex ...

This dialogue lead to 3 different paths and cannot be undone. We'll do all the content then save the game when we only have this to unlock, it will be much easier to explore all 3 paths that way.

Let's simplify our objective for day 1 to 10. We want money and just money, we're going to avoid all others unnecessary contact from day 1 to 10.

  • Ask mom for money everyday (i'm ashamed .... + chore)
