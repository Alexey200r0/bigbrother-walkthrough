Walkthrough for getting enough money to buy Robe & Bikini before eric is completed at this point.

To see others event refer to characters in the menu (Lisa, Alice, Mom, Eric) .

If you did everything correctly you should have 11/12 opportunities. Check Mom (Eric & Mom (22:00 - 23:00)) for the last one.

Shyness level between below normal and very low.

Lisa intro

  • I'll only speak about event we didn't do during the day 1 - 10 walkthrough
  • Stats :
    • Relationship :
      • Helping her with homework for nothing will increase relation. (Warning, doing so make it impossible to increase authority)
      • Helping her with homework by choosing Let me do it by myself! (without errors) at least 6 times will increase relation.
      • Giving her the bathrobe will increase relation
      • Buying the bikini before Eric will increase relation
    • Authority :
      • Make mistakes with homework, she'll get punished and your authority will increase (only way)
    • Mood :
      • pretty easy, you're kind to her and it will increase, you're not and it will decrease
    • Shyness :
      • basically everytime (okay not all situations but most of them) you see her naked or embarrassed. note: shyness can only decrease.

You reached relation : Warm & good mood ?

Talk when available - You have a strange appearance
  • Outcome if successful :
    • You can now buy candies / chocolate.
